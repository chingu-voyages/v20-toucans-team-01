<RecipeLayout>

- 1 pound [pork](https://www.thespruceeats.com/pig-diagram-and-pork-chart-995306) (thinly sliced and cut into bite-size pieces)
- 2 tablespoons ginger (fresh and grated)
- 3 tablespoons [soy sauce](https://www.thespruceeats.com/types-of-chinese-soy-sauce-694628)
- 2 tablespoons [mirin](https://www.thespruceeats.com/mirin-2031388)
- 1 tablespoon sake
- 1 head cabbage (shredded)

1. Mix soy sauce, mirin, sake, and grated ginger in a bowl.Marinate pork for 5 minutes.
2. Heat some oil in a frying pan.
3. Stir-fry the pork slices on medium heat until cooked through.
4. Serve ginger pork with shredded cabbage.

</RecipeLayout>
