- 1/4 ounce Yeast
- 1/4 cup Water
- 1 1/2 cups Water
- 1/2 cup Sugar
- 1 tsp Salt
- 2 Eggs
- 1/3 cup Shortening
- 5 drops Flour
- Sprinking Canola Oil
- 1/2 cup Milk
- 2 cups Sugar
- 1 1/2 cups Vanilla
- 6 tablespoons Boiling Water
- 1/3 cup Butter

1. Dissolve yeast in warm water in 2 1/2-quart bowl.
1. Add milk, sugar, salt, eggs, shortening and 2 cups flour.
1. Beat on low for 30 seconds, scraping bowl constantly.
1. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes, scraping bowl occasionally.
1. Stir in remaining flour until smooth.
1. Cover and let rise until double, 50-60 minutes. (Dough is ready when indentation remains when touched.)
1. Turn dough onto floured surface; roll around lightly to coat with flour.
1. Gently roll dough 1/2-inch thick with floured rolling pin.
1. Cut with floured doughnut cutter.
1. Cover and let rise until double, 30-40 minutes.
1. Heat vegetable oil in deep fryer to 350Â°.
1. Slide doughnuts into hot oil with wide spatula.
1. Turn doughnuts as they rise to the surface.
1. Fry until golden brown, about 1 minute on each side.
1. Remove carefully from oil (do not prick surface); drain.
1. Dip the doughnuts into creamy glaze set on rack.
1. Glaze: Heat butter until melted.
1. Remove from heat.
1. Stir in powdered sugar and vanilla until smooth.
1. Stir in water, 1 tablespoon at a time, until desired consistency.
