<RecipeLayout>

- 25g Bulgur Wheat
- 500g Lamb Mince
- 1 tsp Cumin
- 1 tsp Coriander
- 1 tsp Paprika
- 1 clove finely chopped Garlic
- For frying Olive Oil
- 4 Bun
- Grated Cucumber
- 200g Greek Yogurt
- 2 tbs Mint

1. Tip the bulghar into a pan, cover with water and boil for 10 mins.
1. Drain really well in a sieve, pressing out any excess water.
1. To make the tzatziki, squeeze and discard the juice from the cucumber, then mix into the yogurt with the chopped mint and a little salt.
1. Work the bulghar into the lamb with the spices, garlic (if using) and seasoning, then shape into 4 burgers.
1. Brush with a little oil and fry or barbecue for about 5 mins each side until cooked all the way through.
1. Serve in the buns (toasted if you like) with the tzatziki, tomatoes, onion and a few mint leaves.

</RecipeLayout>
