<RecipeLayout>

- Dash Vegetable Oil
- 1 Can Beef Gravy
- 5 thin cut Potatoes
- 2 cups Cheese Curds

1. Heat oil in a deep fryer or deep heavy skillet to 365°F (185°C).
1. Warm gravy in saucepan or microwave.
1. Place the fries into the hot oil, and cook until light brown, about 5 minutes.
1. Remove to a paper towel lined plate to drain.
1. Place the fries on a serving platter, and sprinkle the cheese over them.
1. Ladle gravy over the fries and cheese, and serve immediately.

</RecipeLayout>
